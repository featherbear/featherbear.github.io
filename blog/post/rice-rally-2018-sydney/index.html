RICE Rally 2018 Sydney

Posted Aug 18, 2018

In a world full of noise, how do we find what we’re really looking for?

RICE Movement is a Christian organisation that brings the message of Jesus to high schoolers (of Australia, and now New Zealand too!)
RICE Rally is their annual event, a big evangelical ‘rally’.

I got the opportunity to serve with my brother in the Production team (that is, lyrics and projection).

Our playground was three LED walls and two confidence monitors.

LED Walls - Left, Centre, Right; Left and Right screens cloned (so two discrete displays)
Confidence Monitors - Two cloned displays
